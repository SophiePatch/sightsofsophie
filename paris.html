Paris, France

The City of Light — a weekend of croissants, art, and golden-hour walks by the Seine.

Through the Streets of Paris

Just a two-hour train ride from London, Paris felt like stepping into a painting. From sunrise over the Seine to twinkling nights at the Eiffel Tower, every moment had its own kind of magic.

My favorite moments were simple — a croissant from a corner bakery, people-watching in the Jardin du Luxembourg, and wandering Montmartre’s cobblestone streets. Paris taught me to slow down, look up, and soak it all in.

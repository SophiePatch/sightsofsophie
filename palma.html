Palma de Mallorca, Spain

Sunshine, sea breezes, and late-night laughter — my Mediterranean escape.

Weekend on the Island

After months in London’s hustle, Palma was a breath of sea air. The island was bright and laid-back — where time slowed and every day felt like golden hour.

My friends and I wandered cobblestone streets lined with palm trees, stopped for tapas near the marina, and spent entire afternoons sitting by the water. Palma reminded me how important it is to pause — to let stillness be part of the adventure.

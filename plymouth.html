Plymouth, England

A coastal retreat of harbors, family memories, and seaside adventures.

A Place That Feels Like Home

My dad’s family lives in Plymouth, so I’ve been visiting since I was only a few months old. Over the years, it’s become a special place filled with both nostalgia and discovery — from running along the Hoe as a kid to finding new cafés and coastal trails as I got older.

Plymouth has that perfect mix of seaside calm and everyday life. Whether you’re watching sailboats from the Barbican, grabbing a pasty from a corner shop, or hiking along the coast, it always feels peaceful and familiar.
